Design a premium desktop-first homepage UI for an enterprise-grade AI-powered GST intelligence platform intended for presentation to senior GST council members, government stakeholders, and highly qualified cost accountancy professionals. The interface should feel sophisticated, institutional, calm, and futuristic without resembling a startup SaaS dashboard or a generic chatbot website. Use a strict light-mode aesthetic with a refined teal and muted gold color palette. The overall emotional tone should communicate trust, governance, intelligence, legal rigor, and national-scale infrastructure. The design should feel spacious, cinematic, elegant, and meticulously structured, with extremely strong typography hierarchy, generous whitespace, soft layered shadows, subtle gradients, glass-like panels, thin gold accents, and restrained premium animations. Avoid clutter, dense dashboards, excessive cards, dark mode, loud gradients, or playful startup visuals.

The homepage should begin with a large immersive hero section that feels like a centralized “GST Intelligence Core.” At the top should be a minimal elegant navigation bar with a sophisticated logo placeholder on the left, navigation items including Litigation, Advisory, Knowledge Repository, Governance, and About in the center, and premium rounded CTA buttons on the right labeled “Launch Assistant” and “Upload Notice.” The hero itself should feature a small institutional label reading “AI-Enabled GST Intelligence Infrastructure” above a large cinematic headline such as “Structured GST Intelligence Across Litigation, Advisory & Compliance.” Supporting text below should explain that the platform consists of three specialized AI systems designed for GST professionals, departmental stakeholders, and institutional tax functions, grounded in traceable legal reasoning and governed workflows.

At the center of the hero should be a large floating AI query interface with elegant rounded corners, subtle teal glow effects, layered translucent surfaces, and a premium glassmorphism treatment used very sparingly and tastefully. The input should feel intelligent and high-end, not like a normal chat input. The placeholder text should rotate through prompts such as “Analyse Show Cause Notice,” “Check ITC Eligibility,” “Retrieve Relevant Circulars,” and “Structure Reply Framework.” Around the interface, softly floating legal references, citations, and GST sections should appear subtly in the background, creating the feeling of structured legal intelligence. On one side of the hero, show a sophisticated AI analysis preview panel containing a GST query, retrieved statutory references, circulars, structured legal reasoning, and an expandable “View Supporting References” interaction. Include elegant badges like “Human Review Recommended,” “Citation Grounded,” and “Audit Trail Enabled.” The hero should feel alive through subtle motion and ambient intelligence rather than flashy animation.

The most important interaction on the homepage should be a beautiful morph transition where the single central GST Intelligence Core gradually transforms into three specialized intelligence systems while scrolling. The morph must feel fluid, continuous, and cinematic, similar to Apple-level or Linear-inspired transitions. The single central AI panel should stretch and elegantly branch into three interconnected intelligence pathways rather than abruptly becoming separate cards. Shared UI elements, flowing lines, glowing legal references, and soft motion trails should visually connect the systems together so they feel like divisions of one governed intelligence architecture. Preserve continuity during the transition by animating typography positions, expanding layouts, splitting the central query bar into three, and allowing floating citations and legal references to flow into each specialized system.

After the morph transition, reveal three large premium intelligence panels arranged horizontally with substantial spacing and connected by subtle visual lines and gradients. The first and visually dominant panel should represent Litigation Intelligence using deeper teal tones and refined gold accents. This panel should contain content about analysing show cause notices, retrieving judicial precedents, validating procedural compliance, and structuring defence workflows. Include elegant animated previews such as SCN uploads, extracted allegations, section references, case law retrieval, and structured rebuttal generation. The second panel should represent Advisory Intelligence using lighter teal gradients and sophisticated transaction-analysis visuals such as multi-party GST structures, ITC assessment flows, place-of-supply diagrams, and contract alignment previews. The third panel should represent Operational Intelligence using softer cyan-teal accents and visuals for GST rate retrieval, ERP tax guidance, classification support, SOP references, and standardized compliance responses. Each panel should include elegant rounded CTA buttons like “Open Litigation Assistant,” “Open Advisory Assistant,” and “Open Operational Assistant.”

Below the three systems, continue the homepage with an institutional governance section emphasizing trust, traceability, and professional oversight. Use elegant layered cards with thin gold borders and soft teal shadows. Include governance principles such as Human-in-the-Loop Review, Complete Audit Trails, Curated GST Knowledge Boundaries, Role-Based Access Controls, and Enterprise AI Infrastructure. The content should emphasize that all outputs remain reviewable, auditable, citation-grounded, and professionally governed. After this, include a sophisticated knowledge repository section visualizing GST Acts, Rules, Circulars, Notifications, Internal Tax Positions, Commercial Documentation, Litigation History, and Judicial References flowing into the AI intelligence core. This section should visually communicate that the platform operates only on curated controlled GST knowledge and not generic internet data.

Further down the page, include a clean visual workflow section showing the progression from Query Submission to Repository Retrieval, Legal Ranking, Structured AI Analysis, Citation Validation, Professional Review, and Final Output. Use elegant connected nodes, subtle teal line animations, and restrained motion design. The overall UI should feel like a calm national-scale legal intelligence system rather than a productivity app. The design language should be highly polished, editorial, futuristic, and premium, with an emphasis on composure, institutional trust, and governed AI-assisted GST reasoning.
